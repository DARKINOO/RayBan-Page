Who Are We

XANANOIDS, the club by robotic people of JECRC College, is the group of students who works and are interested in the field of robotics. It was founded in 2004. Since the beginning, students of this club are developing various educational robot, with this, the members of this club are taking participation and winning various titles and awards at different IITs, NITs, ABU ASIA-PACIFIC ROBOCON CONTEST and various regional institutions.

Objective of the club is to deal with the behaviour based robotics, AI, software, 3-D designing also the main idea behind the formation of the club is to develop cost effective educational robotic platform for students. Currently, the club has a team of 40 members. All the members are engineering students of various branches from different semesters and working on development of various industry level projects.
