Welcome to the Xananoids!

The premier robotics club at our college where innovation and technology converge! At Xananoids, we are passionate about designing and building robots that compete in exhilarating events such as Robo War, Game of Drones, Robo-Soccer, and Formula Zero. Our club thrives on creativity and technical excellence, pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in robotics. But that’s not all—Xananoids is also a hub for developing cutting-edge gadgets and electronic innovations. Our members work on a variety of projects, from smart devices to advanced automation systems, showcasing their prowess in electronics. We are at the forefront of software development, creating sophisticated programs and applications that drive our robotic and electronic creations. Whether you're a hardware enthusiast, a software developer, or both, Xananoids offers a collaborative environment to hone your skills and turn your ideas into reality. Join us at Xananoids and be part of a community dedicated to pioneering advancements in robotics and technology!
