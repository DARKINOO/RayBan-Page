Formula zero

Welcome to Formula Zero, the ultimate showdown of engineering prowess and innovation at our college fest! This electrifying competition pits the brightest minds against each other, challenging participants to design and build hand-made robots that can navigate through intricate pathways and overcome various hurdles. Whether your bot is wired or wireless, the thrill of seeing it tackle obstacles, maneuver through tight turns, and outsmart the competition is unparalleled. Join us for a day of excitement, creativity, and cutting-edge technology as we celebrate the future of robotics. Get ready to witness the ingenuity and determination of our talented engineers in a race to the finish line!

Xan Expo

Every year, the Xananoids Robotics Club proudly hosts a dynamic exhibition showcasing self-made robotics projects in both hardware and software. This highly anticipated event is a celebration of innovation and creativity, bringing together talented students from across the college to demonstrate their cutting-edge inventions. Attendees are treated to a diverse array of projects, from autonomous vehicles and robotic arms to intelligent software applications and interactive systems. The exhibition not only highlights the technical prowess of our members but also fosters a spirit of collaboration and learning, inspiring future engineers and technologists.
